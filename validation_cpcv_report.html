Methodology Notes

CPCV (López de Prado 2018): C(N,k) combinatorial splits from N=12 non-overlapping blocks, k=2 held out per path → C(12,2)=66 paths × 2 seeds = 132 total paths. Purge gap: 20 trading days on each test boundary.

Deflated Sharpe (Bailey & López de Prado 2014): Adjusts raw Sharpe for selection bias from testing ~250 strategy variants. DSR > 0 means strategy likely survives multiple-testing.

PBO (Bailey-Borwein-LdP-Zhu 2017): Probability of Backtest Overfitting. PBO < 0.10 = low overfitting risk. PBO > 0.30 = strategy likely overfit to historical data.

Signal inclusion threshold: p p < 0.15lt; 0.15 (Harvey-Liu-Zhu 2016 recommend p < 0.003 for academic publication; p < 0.05 is a practical compromise for live trading systems).
